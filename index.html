📚 APA 7th Quick Rules
In-text: one author
(Smith, 2022, p. 45)(Smith 2022 p45)
In-text: two authors
(Smith & Jones, 2022)(Smith and Jones, 2022)
Three or more authors
(Smith et al., 2022)(Smith, Jones & Brown, 2022)
